Why Did I Create This Course

I noticed students struggling with rote memorization; it's painful and inefficient. I want to give you the "source code" to help you decode the logic between phonetic and semantic components.

When you learn one character, you are entering a network.

  • Phonetic components give you sound clues.
  • Semantic components guide meaning.

When you understand the system, you begin to see patterns. You will learn how characters are built, how components work, and how to recognize patterns that make learning significantly easier.

Why Do You Need This Course

If you want:

  • Efficiency: Master one phonetic component to predict dozens of characters.
  • Logic Over Rote: Understand the "why" to make memory effortless.
  • Reading Intuition: Develop the intuition to guess the meanings and sounds of new words.
